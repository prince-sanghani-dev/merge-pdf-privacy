Privacy Policy

Merge PDF · com.prince.mergepdf

Effective date: April 17, 2026  |  Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Merge PDF ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Merge PDF is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • The content of your PDF files
  • Your location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers

2. How the App Works

All PDF merging, compression, watermarking, and file management happens locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to any server. The app reads PDF files you select and writes the merged output back to your device storage — nothing leaves your phone.

3. Storage Access

The app requests access to your device storage solely to:

  • Read the PDF files you choose to merge
  • Save the merged PDF output to your device

We do not access any other files on your device beyond those you explicitly select.

4. Internet Access

The app requests internet permission to support the following features:

  • Opening your browser to the Play Store for rating or sharing the app
  • Sending feedback you write (if you choose to submit it)

No PDF content or personal data is ever sent over the internet.

5. Third-Party Services

The current version of Merge PDF does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Future updates may add services such as Google AdMob (advertising) and RevenueCat (in-app purchases). This policy will be updated before any such integration is released.

6. Children's Privacy

Merge PDF is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Data Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. App preferences (such as your sort order or favourites list) are stored locally on your device using Android's standard storage APIs and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy while using this app, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
